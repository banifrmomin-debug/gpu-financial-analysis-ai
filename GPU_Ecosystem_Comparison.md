## GPU Ecosystem Overview

The semiconductor ecosystem is shifting from general-purpose CPUs toward integrated **AI factories**, where compute, memory, networking, and software are optimized together to run large AI workloads. ## Ecosystem Roles

**Designers**  
NVIDIA, AMD, and Marvell are fabless semiconductor companies that design complex System-on-Chip (SoC) architectures and AI accelerators.

**Foundries**  
TSMC and Samsung manufacture advanced chips using leading fabrication processes such as 7nm and smaller nodes.

**Memory Suppliers**  
Micron and SK hynix provide high-bandwidth memory (HBM), which is critical for feeding massive AI models with data at extremely high speeds.
